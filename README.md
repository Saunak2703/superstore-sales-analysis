# Superstore Sales Analysis

## 1. Project Overview
Analysis of 9,994 sales records from a US Superstore 
to identify key business insights and revenue patterns.

## 2. Tools Used
1. Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn)
2. SQL (sqlite3)
3. Dataset: Sample Superstore (Kaggle)

## 3. Business Questions Answered
1. Which region has highest sales?
2. Which category is most profitable?
3. What are top 10 products by revenue?
4. Which customer segment generates most sales?
5. What is the monthly sales trend across years?

## 4. Key Insights
1. West region generates highest sales ($725,457)
2. Technology is most profitable category ($145,454)
3. Consumer segment dominates with 50.6% of sales
4. November consistently highest sales month every year
5. Furniture has high sales but very low profit margin
6. Sales growing year on year from 2014 to 2017
